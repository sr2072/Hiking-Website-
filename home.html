Welcome to BetterTrails!

BetterTrails is a site dedicated to helping the adventurous outdoors-men and women find their next favorite hiking trail!

> > >

The Joy of the Journey

At its core, BetterTrails is a community based platform. BetterTrails was initially created by a group of likeminded individuals who were interested in getting out and seeing some more of the world. The only problem we found was that the world was sometimes a little hard to navigate. So, we decided to band together and help ourselves and the rest of the outdoors community do more of what they love and help others do the same. With BetterTrails you can find and make some of the following content listed below.

Maps
Discuss, rate and make a trail map. Google maps support soon to be implemented too, helping others find their way to your favorite spots!
Events
With an upcoming social system, BetterTrails will be working with organizations recognized the world over to help you and others host trail cleanup and nature aid events. Soon, you and your friends will be able to make that poorly managed trail you've always loved become a beautiful new landmark, or sign up to help other events in your own local community!
User Profiles
As part of the upcoming social system update, BetterTrails is implementing one of the most unique and fun-oriented social systems on the market! Discuss and show off your gear, rise up the ranks by completing more difficult trails, and get unique badges to show off your various accomplishments! Whether you're an avid hiker, more into the cleanup of your community or just looking for some occasional walking in a peaceful place, your profile will make it that much more fun!

Forever Free, Forever Fun!

As part of our mission to bringing the joy of the outdoors to as many people as possible, our team at BetterTrails is dedicated to keeping your experience hiking free from any and all ads and intrusive stuff. We are and will forever be open and free to any who are interested in the open world!
